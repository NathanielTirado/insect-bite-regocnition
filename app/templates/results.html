Thoose bratty bed bugs!

Bed Bugs are most commonly found in areas where people rest or sleep, such as:

  • Furniture
  • Beds
  • The inside seams of furniture

Most commonly, you'd get a bed bug bite at:

  • Hotels
  • Nursing homes
  • Schools and daycare centers
  • Hospitals
  • College Dorms
  • Public Transportation

Alright, how do we remedy a bug bite?

  • Calamine Lotion, Non Prescription Hydrocortisone or any Anti-Itch Cream
  • Tea Tree Oil
  • Cool compress
  • Oral Antihistamine
